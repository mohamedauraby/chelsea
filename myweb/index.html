Ice cold

This is a very quick show of my favourite game I have ever been to.

Let me take you on a short trip on how a meaningless game had me tearing up like this by the end of it

Great start

We started flying. Gallagher smashes one in early and the place just explodes; I'm shouting my head off. Then Palmer steps up for a penalty, calm as ever, and makes it 2. At that point I'm thinking, 'this is going to be an easy night.'

Back to reality

Then it all turns. We mess up at the back and Garnacho punishes us, and before I can even catch my breath Fernandes heads one in. From 2-0 up to 2-2; I'm just standing there, half angry, half stunned, thinking, 'here we go again.'

It gets worse...

Second half, and it somehow gets worse. Antony trivelas into the box and Garnacho pops up again to make it 3-2. I just sat there in disbelief, can't lie - felt like we'd completely thrown it away.

Salvage a point?

Then the madness kicks in. Deep in stoppage time we get a penalty, and Palmer steps up. I can barely watch, he buries it, and Stamford Bridge goes absolutely nuts. I'm jumping around like I've lost my mind.

Absolute Madness

Seconds later, it happens - the ball falls to Palmer again, he hits it, deflects in. I lose it completely. People are on chairs, hugging strangers, pure chaos. A moment so good a picture wouldnt do it justice, so here's a video of it!
